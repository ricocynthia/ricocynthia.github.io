I'm a senior full stack software engineer with 6 years of experience building and shipping high-traffic, user-facing systems at scale. I'm especially motivated by working on systems that people rely on every day — where reliability and thoughtful user experience really matter.

I like understanding how a product works end-to-end: from the frontend experience all the way through backend services and APIs. International Document Verification
TypeScript Node.js Go Jumio gRPC

International travelers needed a way to verify travel documents without calling the airport. I built and owned the document verification microsite end-to-end — integrating with Jumio's identity verification API, designing the backend service contracts, and handling complex edge cases in the guest-facing flow. The result: a fully self-service experience that reduced operational support load.

React TypeScript Node.js BFF Go

Group bookings had no self-service check-in path, pushing travelers to call centers and congesting airports. I led and delivered group reservation check-in and enhanced search APIs — implementing passenger-level authorization, supporting both migrated and legacy reservation formats, and coordinating across backend teams to ship a reliable experience at scale.
